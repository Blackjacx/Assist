OVERVIEW: Access FCM, see https://firebase.google.com/docs/cloud-messaging

USAGE: push fcm [--verbose ...] --device-token <device-token> --message <message> --service-account-json-path <service-account-json-path>

OPTIONS:
  -v, --verbose           Activate verbose logging.
  -d, --device-token <device-token>
                          The token of the device you want to push to.
  -m, --message <message> The message you want to send.
  --service-account-json-path <service-account-json-path>
                          The path to your service-account json file.
  --version               Show the version.
  -h, --help              Show help information.
