OVERVIEW: A utility for accessing the App Store Connect API.

USAGE: asc <subcommand>

OPTIONS:
  --version               Show the version.
  -h, --help              Show help information.

SUBCOMMANDS:
  keys                    Lists, registers and deletes App Store Connect API
                          keys on your Mac.
  beta-groups (default)   Manage groups of beta testers that have access to one
                          or more builds.
  apps                    Manage your apps in App Store Connect.
  app-store-versions      Manage versions of your app that are available in App
                          Store.
  beta-testers            Manage people who can install and test prerelease
                          builds.
  builds                  Manage builds for testers and submit builds for
                          review.
  bundle-ids              Manage the bundle IDs that uniquely identify your
                          apps.

  See 'asc help <subcommand>' for detailed help.
