OVERVIEW: A utility for sending and testing push notifications to Apple Push
Notification Service (APNS) and via Firebase.

USAGE: push <subcommand>

OPTIONS:
  --version               Show the version.
  -h, --help              Show help information.

SUBCOMMANDS:
  apns (default)          Access APNS, see
                          https://developer.apple.com/documentation/usernotifications/setting_up_a_remote_notification_server/establishing_a_token-based_connection_to_apns
  fcm                     Access FCM, see
                          https://firebase.google.com/docs/cloud-messaging

  See 'push help <subcommand>' for detailed help.
