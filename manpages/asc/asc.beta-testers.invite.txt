OVERVIEW: Send or resend an invitation to a beta tester to test specified apps.

USAGE: asc beta-testers invite [--verbose ...] [--key-id <key-id>] [--app-ids <app-ids> ...] --email <email>

OPTIONS:
  -v, --verbose           Activate verbose logging.
  -k, --key-id <key-id>   The ID of the key to use.
  -i, --app-ids <app-ids> The Apple app ID(s) that uniquely identifiy the
                          app(s) (e.g. -i "12345678" "14324567").
  -e, --email <email>     The unique email of the tester to send the invite to.
  --version               Show the version.
  -h, --help              Show help information.
