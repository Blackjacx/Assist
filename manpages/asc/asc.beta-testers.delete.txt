OVERVIEW: Remove a beta tester's ability to test all or specific apps.

USAGE: asc beta-testers delete [--verbose ...] [--key-id <key-id>] [--filters <filters> ...]

OPTIONS:
  -v, --verbose           Activate verbose logging.
  -k, --key-id <key-id>   The ID of the key to use.
  -f, --filters <filters> Filter used for searching for the user. See
                          https://developer.apple.com/documentation/appstoreconnectapi/list_beta_testers
                          for possible values.
  --version               Show the version.
  -h, --help              Show help information.
