OVERVIEW: Create a beta tester assigned to a group, a build, or an app.

USAGE: asc beta-testers add [--verbose ...] [--key-id <key-id>] --first-name <first-name> --last-name <last-name> --email <email> --group-names <group-names> ...

OPTIONS:
  -v, --verbose           Activate verbose logging.
  -k, --key-id <key-id>   The ID of the key to use.
  -n, --first-name <first-name>
                          The first name of the user.
  -l, --last-name <last-name>
                          The last name of the user.
  -e, --email <email>     The email of the user.
  -g, --group-names <group-names>
                          The group names to add the new beta tester to.
  --version               Show the version.
  -h, --help              Show help information.
